Critical gaps
47 of 60 cities have zero programs scheduled
Only 13 cities are currently active. Activating the dormant 47 with even 1 IE cohort/month each would add tens of thousands of completions annually.
Entire US West Coast is dark
San Francisco, San Diego, Portland, Sacramento, Orange County — all have zero programs. Combined metro population: ~20M. Largest unserved market in NA.
11 Midwest cities with zero IE or Hatha Yoga
Chicago, Columbus, Indianapolis, Milwaukee, Detroit, Cleveland, Dayton, Cincinnati, St. Louis, Kansas City, Minneapolis — all dormant despite active city centers.
Nashville: 45 min from Isha Institute TN — zero programs
The city closest to Isha's flagship US campus has no scheduled programs. Extraordinary gap given proximity to teachers, materials, and support infrastructure.
Opportunities
Toronto model: replicate in 10 major metros
Toronto runs 3 IE programs + 12 Hatha Yoga across 6 sub-cities with a single coordination hub. This model applied to Chicago, SF, Houston, DC, and LA could add 50K+ annually.
Philadelphia is the most programmatically rich US city
9 scheduled programs at a single recurring venue (Exton Hotel). This proves sustained multi-format city programming is operationally achievable in the US.
Corporate B2B: largest untapped multiplier
Chicago, NYC, SF, Houston, Dallas, DC — massive corporate markets with zero active programs. One Work Beyond Stress contract with a Fortune 500 = hundreds of IE participants, employer-subsidized.
Canada: Toronto + Vancouver prove the model works
Both are highly active with multi-format calendars. Calgary, Montreal, Edmonton, Ottawa, and Regina have zero programs — ready for the same playbook.
